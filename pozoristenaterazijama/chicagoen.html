CHICAGO
Director: Kokan Mladenović


The plot of the musical "Chicago" takes place in the same notorious American city in the twenties. In the center of action is the irresistible blonde Roxy, a bar singer not-yet-discovered and a woman who killed her lover, because she " can't be left by no one!" When she arrived to jail she meets the 'famous' bar singer, brunette and truly "killer" Velma (who killed her sister and her husband when she found them in an adultery) - and other prisoners, mostly murderers of unfaithful men. Velma is defended by a famous lawyer who, when takes care of his clients only if the defendant has 5,000 for his fee. When Roxy agrees to the amount of his fees, he steps into an uncompromising manipulation of both the media and of her...
