SOME LIKE IT HOT
Directors: Rade Marjanović i Svetislav Goncić


For the longest played production at the Terazije Theatre, the main "culprit" is, of course, its first director, Soja Jovanovic, who had the courage and skill to successfully engage in the realization of "Some like It Hot ", after the eponymous hit movie and Broadway, and on the local scene. The story of two musicians, who dress for their jobsas women, a fairy tale blonde thats about a "millionaire" and a real millionaire who falls in love with a man, is in fact, a story about the triumph of love and beauty. Best support of the Hollywood scene are glamorous scenery and costumes, great musical arrangements and choreography, and a keen sense of the protagonist's comic play ...
